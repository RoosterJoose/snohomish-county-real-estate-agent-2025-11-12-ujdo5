Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and track your activity on our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our website and improve performance
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices to enhance your experience
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity to deliver targeted advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness

Other Tracking Technologies

We also use other tracking technologies such as:

  • Web Beacons and Pixels: Transparent images used to track page visits and user activity
  • Analytics Tools: Services like Google Analytics to measure website traffic and user behavior
  • Advertising Networks: Third-party networks that track your activity across websites for targeted advertising

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can typically delete cookies and disable future cookie storage through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website. You can also opt out of targeted advertising through industry opt-out mechanisms.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure socket layer (SSL) technology for website communications
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You use our services at your own risk.

For example, we may retain transaction records for the duration required by law, while marketing data may be retained until you unsubscribe. If you request deletion of your information, we will remove it from our active systems, though we may retain archival copies as required by law.
